Wolves need our help.

Defend the wild. Fight for wolves.

Women for Wolves is a non-profit organization that fights to protect wild wolves and provide sanctuary for wolf-dogs.

We are a 501(c)3 non-profit, wolf-dog sanctuary, and a USDA Licensed Facility. We are not open to the public currently—check our events page for upcoming events or hikes with our rescues.

About Us

Wolf Coexistence Program

Protecting Wolves, Supporting Communities, Building Tolerance.

We partner with the Cry Wolf Project to deliver non-lethal deterrents that safeguard wolves, foster community involvement, and provide essential education and resources.
